Discover UMD & Duluth

Choosing the right university is one of the most important decisions a student can make. For me, the University of Minnesota Duluth stood out as the perfect place to pursue my passion for finance and accounting while experiencing the unique beauty and opportunities that Duluth has to offer.

Why I Chose University of Minnesota Duluth

Academic Excellence

When I began my college search, I was looking for a university with a strong business program that would prepare me for a career in finance and accounting. UMD's Labovitz School of Business and Economics immediately caught my attention. The program's reputation for academic rigor, combined with its focus on practical, real-world applications, made it the ideal choice for my educational goals.

What particularly appealed to me was UMD's commitment to small class sizes and personalized attention. Unlike larger universities where students can feel lost in the crowd, UMD offers an environment where professors know your name and are genuinely invested in your success. This personalized approach has been invaluable in my academic journey, allowing me to maintain a 3.96 GPA while actively engaging with course material and building relationships with faculty members.

Value and Affordability

As a finance student, I understand the importance of making sound financial decisions. UMD offers exceptional value for the quality of education it provides. The university's commitment to keeping education affordable while maintaining high academic standards made it an intelligent investment in my future. The opportunity to receive a top-tier education without accumulating excessive student debt was a significant factor in my decision.

Career Opportunities

UMD's strong connections with regional and national employers in the business and finance sectors provide excellent internship and career opportunities. The university's location in Duluth, a regional economic hub, means that students have access to internships and networking opportunities with companies in finance, accounting, and business consulting. This proximity to potential employers has been crucial as I seek internship opportunities to gain hands-on experience in my field.

Supportive Community

From my first campus visit, I was struck by the welcoming and supportive atmosphere at UMD. The sense of community is palpable—students support each other, faculty members are approachable and helpful, and the campus itself feels like a close-knit community. This supportive environment has been essential to my success, providing me with the resources and encouragement I need to excel academically and personally.

What I Love About Duluth and the Surrounding Area

Natural Beauty

Duluth's location on the shores of Lake Superior is nothing short of spectacular. The natural beauty of the area provides a perfect balance to academic life. Whether I'm taking a break from studying to walk along the Lakewalk, watching freighters navigate the canal, or simply enjoying the breathtaking sunsets over the lake, Duluth's natural environment offers constant inspiration and opportunities for relaxation.

The changing seasons in Duluth are particularly special. Fall brings incredible colors as the leaves change against the backdrop of Lake Superior. Winter transforms the area into a snowy wonderland, while spring and summer offer perfect weather for outdoor activities. This connection to nature has been important for maintaining balance in my life as a student.

Canal Park and Waterfront

Canal Park is one of my favorite places in Duluth. The historic Aerial Lift Bridge, the shipping canal, and the vibrant waterfront area create a unique atmosphere that you won't find anywhere else. I love watching the massive freighters pass through the canal—it's a reminder of the global connections and economic activity that make Duluth a special place. The area also offers great restaurants, shops, and events that provide fun ways to relax and connect with friends.

Outdoor Recreation

Duluth is a paradise for anyone who enjoys outdoor activities. The Lakewalk provides miles of scenic trails perfect for walking, running, or biking. The area offers excellent opportunities for hiking, with numerous trails in and around Duluth. Whether I want to go for a hike in one of the many parks, spend time at the beach, or explore the surrounding North Shore, there's always something to do that gets me outside and active.

Cultural and Entertainment Opportunities

Despite being a smaller city, Duluth offers a rich cultural scene with museums, theaters, music venues, and festivals throughout the year. The city has a vibrant arts community and hosts numerous events that provide entertainment and cultural enrichment. This blend of natural beauty and cultural opportunities creates a well-rounded living experience that enhances my time as a student.

Sense of Community

Duluth has a strong sense of community that I find welcoming and supportive. The city's residents are friendly and engaged, creating an environment where students feel like they belong. This community spirit extends to the relationship between UMD and the city, with strong connections that benefit both students and residents.

Personal Experiences and Campus Highlights

First Impressions

My first visit to UMD left a lasting impression. Walking across the beautiful campus, seeing the modern facilities, and feeling the energy of students going about their day, I knew this was where I belonged. The campus strikes the perfect balance between having state-of-the-art facilities and maintaining a welcoming, accessible atmosphere.

Academic Experiences

My academic experience at UMD has exceeded my expectations. The finance and accounting courses are challenging yet engaging, with professors who bring real-world experience into the classroom. One of my favorite aspects has been the opportunity to apply theoretical concepts to practical scenarios, preparing me for the challenges I'll face in my career.

The support I've received from faculty members has been exceptional. Whether I need clarification on a concept, advice on course selection, or guidance on career planning, my professors have always been willing to help. This level of support has been instrumental in my academic success and personal growth.

Campus Life

UMD's campus offers the perfect environment for both studying and socializing. The library provides excellent study spaces, from quiet individual study areas to collaborative group workspaces. The student union serves as a hub of activity, offering places to eat, study, and connect with other students.

I've also appreciated the variety of student organizations and activities available on campus. While I'm focused on my studies, having opportunities to get involved in clubs and activities has helped me build a well-rounded college experience and develop leadership and teamwork skills.

Memorable Moments

Some of my most memorable moments at UMD have come from the small, everyday experiences—studying with classmates in the library, having insightful discussions with professors after class, and taking breaks to enjoy the beautiful campus surroundings. These moments have created a sense of belonging and made UMD feel like home.

I've also enjoyed exploring Duluth with friends, whether it's watching ships in the canal, hiking local trails, or simply enjoying the natural beauty of the area. These experiences have enriched my college experience and created lasting memories.

Looking Forward

As I continue my journey at UMD, I'm excited about the opportunities that lie ahead. I'm looking forward to taking more advanced courses in my major, participating in internships, and continuing to explore all that Duluth has to offer. UMD has provided me with an excellent foundation for my future career, and I'm grateful for the experiences and growth I've already achieved here.

For anyone considering UMD, I would encourage them to visit the campus and experience for themselves what makes this university special. The combination of academic excellence, beautiful surroundings, supportive community, and exciting opportunities creates an exceptional college experience that I'm proud to be a part of.
